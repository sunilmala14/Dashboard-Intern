App Channel Dominates — but Diversification is Overdue
The App channel drives 42.7% (₹78,386) of total revenue and handles 15 of 51 orders — nearly 3× Amazon's volume. However, this concentration creates platform risk. Instagram (₹44,410) is the rising challenger, showing strong organic pull with only 11 orders but high average basket size of ₹4,037 per order — ideal for Skincare hero launches.
💎
Vitamin C Serum is the Revenue Engine — Protect it
Vitamin C Serum alone contributes ₹30,730 (16.8%) of total revenue across 6 orders — the highest among all 18 SKUs. Combined with Collagen Supplement (₹20,429) and Conditioner (₹14,299), the top 3 products drive 35.7% of revenue. A stockout or rating dip in these hero SKUs would materially impact the P&L.
⚠️
Foundation & Scalp Serum Need a Strategic Review
Foundation (₹133, 1 order) and Scalp Serum (₹2,513) are consistently low-revenue SKUs with sub-50% and 63% margins respectively. Foundation is also the only product with margin below 50%. Given limited shelf life of 365 days, these should be flagged for bundling, promotional pricing, or discontinuation before Q1 2025.
